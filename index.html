In a school with so much to offer, it's normal to get lost, especially if the halls are not labeled. Labeling halls makes it easier to find where you're going, because it assigns a letter to your classes. This site will list all of the halls in the school and rank them out of 6.
It is a unique and funny idea that nobody would really think about doing, so I have taken that task into my own hands.

The halls are labeled A-G, disregarding F. Each of them houses a different subject. Halls C, D, E, and G go upstairs to allow for even more subjects to be taught without extending the school building horizontally.
