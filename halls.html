The halls of this school are listed below:

  • A Hall - Art classes, visual and audial, are taken here. Examples include band, chorus, fine art, digital art, and applied art.
  • B Hall - Most electives take place here. Along with this, the physical education gyms are here.
  • C Hall - Depending on which floor, a variety of classes are taken here. On floor 1, english is here, but on floor 2, science is taken.
  • D Hall - Another floor-dependent hall, this hall contains history and math on floors 1 and 2, respectively.
  • E Hall - On floor 1, english and history are taken. On floor 2, world languages are learned.
  • G Hall - Floor 1 holds history classes and the computer lab while floor 2 has chemistry.
  • H Hall - H Hall is the shortest hall in the school, only teaching health and holding the newer auxiliary gym.

Hall Facts

B Hall does have a part in floor 2, but it is labeled as D Hall. Only three classrooms are in this segment: D203, D202, and D201.

There are stairwells in C Hall, D Hall, E Hall, and G Hall.
